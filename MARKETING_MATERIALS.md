## 📝 Press Release

### Headline
```
منِ فیزیکی (PhysicsMe) Launches: Comprehensive Mobile App 
for Physics Education in Persian
```

### Subheading
```
Free educational app brings interactive learning to Iranian 
high school students with articles, problems, experiments, and community support
```

### Body Text
```
IRAN - PhysicsMe, a comprehensive mobile educational application 
for physics learning, is now available on Google Play Store.

Developed for high school students (Grades 10-12), the app provides:

FEATURES:
• Read Physics Articles - Full-text articles with mathematical equations
• Practice Questions - Interactive Q&A with instant answer verification
• Solve Problems - Comprehensive problem sets with detailed solutions
• Virtual Lab Experiments - Safe, interactive physics demonstrations
• Community Q&A - Ask questions and get help from peers
• Download Articles - Export as PDF for offline reading
• Bookmark System - Save favorite articles for later
• Offline Support - Read cached articles without internet
• Push Notifications - Stay updated on new content
• Persian Language - Full RTL support for Persian speakers

ABOUT THE APP:
PhysicsMe is built with modern web technologies (Vue 3, Capacitor) 
to provide a fast, responsive experience on all Android devices. 
The app requires no account creation and respects user privacy 
with no data collection or tracking.

"We built PhysicsMe to make physics learning accessible, engaging, 
and interactive for students everywhere," said the development team.

AVAILABILITY:
PhysicsMe is free to download on Google Play Store for Android devices 
running SDK 23 (Android 5.3) and higher.

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
• Android 5.3+
• 50 MB free storage
• Internet for initial setup (offline-capable afterward)

LEARN MORE:
• Google Play: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ir.physicalme.app
• Website: https://physicsme.ir
• Contact: hbagheri@glenar.com

###
```

## 💬 Response Templates for Common Questions

### Q: Is the app free?
A: Yes! PhysicsMe is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases. 
   No subscriptions required. Just download and start learning!

### Q: Do I need an account?
A: No account needed! Just download and start using immediately. 
   Your bookmarks and reading history are saved locally on your device.

### Q: Does it work offline?
A: Yes! Once you've read an article online, it's cached and you can 
   read it again offline. You can also download articles as PDF for 
   guaranteed offline access.

### Q: What physics topics are covered?
A: We cover the Iranian high school physics curriculum for Grades 10-12:
   - Mechanics (kinematics, dynamics, energy)
   - Thermodynamics
   - Waves and Sound
   - Electricity and Magnetism
   - Modern Physics

### Q: Can I download articles?
A: Yes! Every article can be downloaded as a PDF and read on any device.

### Q: Is my data private?
A: Absolutely. We don't collect any personal data. No tracking, 
   no analytics, no ads. Everything is stored locally on your device.

### Q: How do I report a bug?
A: Please contact us at hbagheri@glenar.com with details about 
   the issue or leave a detailed review on Google Play Store.

### Q: When will iOS version be available?
A: We're planning an iOS version. Follow us on social media for updates!
