## App Icon Design Guidelines

### Icon Specifications
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Size**: 512 x 512 pixels
- **Safe Zone**: 45 x 45 pixels from edges

### Design Concept
**Option 1: Circle with Book**
- Circle background (#5B6E32 olive green)
- Book icon or layers representing knowledge
- Persian text "فیزیک" or "منِ فیزیکی"

**Option 2: Physics Symbol**
- Atom symbol or physics formula
- Olive green color (#5B6E32)
- Modern, minimalist design
- Clear at small sizes (24x24, 48x48)

**Option 3: Gradient Sphere**
- Physics concept visualization
- Gradient from olive to cream (#FBF6E3)
- Orbital rings or wave patterns
- Modern, educational look

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #5B6E32 (Olive Green)
- **Secondary**: #FBF6E3 (Cream)
- **Accent**: #D4A847 (Gold)
